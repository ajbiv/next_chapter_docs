# Business Analysis & Processes

This folder contains business-oriented documentation, process analysis, and organizational planning materials.

## Contents

- **Business Requirements**: Business needs, objectives, and success criteria
- **Process Documentation**: Workflow analysis and process improvements
- **Stakeholder Analysis**: Stakeholder mapping and communication plans
- **Market Research**: Competitive analysis and market insights
- **Business Cases**: ROI analysis and business justifications
- **Organizational Planning**: Team structure and resource planning

## Naming Convention

Use clear, business-focused naming:
- `business-requirements.md`
- `stakeholder-analysis.md`
- `process-workflow-current-state.md`
- `market-research-findings.md`
