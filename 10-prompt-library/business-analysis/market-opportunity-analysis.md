# Market Opportunity Analysis

**Category**: Business Analysis
**AI Models**: GPT-4, Claude, Perplexity (for current data)
**Difficulty**: Intermediate
**Output Type**: Comprehensive Analysis Report
**Estimated Time**: 15-20 minutes

## Purpose
Generate a comprehensive market opportunity analysis for your venture, including market size, growth trends, competitive landscape, and entry strategy recommendations.

## Business Context Required
- Industry and target market definition
- Product/service description
- Geographic focus
- Target customer segments
- Current business stage

## Pre-Prompt Setup
```
Context: I'm analyzing the market opportunity for [venture description]. We're building [product/service description] for [target customer segment] in the [geographic market]. We're currently in the [stage] stage and considering [specific decision/strategy].
```

## Core Prompt

```
Please conduct a comprehensive market opportunity analysis for my venture. I need you to analyze:

1. **Market Size & Growth**
   - Total Addressable Market (TAM)
   - Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)  
   - Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
   - Historical growth rates and future projections
   - Key growth drivers and constraints

2. **Market Dynamics**
   - Industry trends and disruptions
   - Regulatory environment and changes
   - Technology impacts and adoption rates
   - Economic factors affecting the market

3. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Direct and indirect competitors
   - Market share distribution
   - Competitive positioning and differentiation
   - Barriers to entry and competitive moats

4. **Customer Analysis**
   - Customer segments and personas
   - Buying behavior and decision processes
   - Pain points and unmet needs
   - Price sensitivity and willingness to pay

5. **Opportunity Assessment**
   - Market gaps and white spaces
   - Timing and market readiness
   - Go-to-market strategy recommendations
   - Revenue potential and business model fit

Please provide specific data points where possible, note your confidence levels, and highlight areas where additional research would be valuable. Format your response with clear sections and actionable insights.
```

## Follow-up Prompts

1. **Deep Dive**: "Can you elaborate on the [specific market segment/competitor/trend] and provide more detailed analysis of how it affects our opportunity?"

2. **Validation**: "What specific data sources or research methods would you recommend to validate these market insights?"

3. **Strategy**: "Based on this analysis, what would be the optimal market entry strategy and timeline?"

4. **Risk Assessment**: "What are the main market risks and how could market conditions change to affect this opportunity?"

- **Perplexity**: Best for current market data and recent trends
