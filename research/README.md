# Research

This folder contains all research and analysis activities, including market research, competitive analysis, and AI-generated insights.

## Purpose
Centralized repository for all research activities that inform business decisions, strategy development, and market understanding. This includes both traditional research and AI-assisted analysis.

## Folder Structure

```
research/
├── README.md              # This file
├── market/               # Market research and industry analysis
├── competitive/          # Competitive analysis and positioning
├── customer/            # Customer research and insights
├── ai/                  # AI-generated research and analysis
├── trends/              # Industry trends and future analysis
└── synthesis/           # Combined insights and cross-research analysis
```

## Contents

### Market Research (`market/`)
- Market sizing and opportunity analysis
- Industry trends and dynamics
- Regulatory environment analysis
- Economic factors and market conditions
- Geographic market analysis

### Competitive Analysis (`competitive/`)
- Competitor research and profiling
- Competitive positioning analysis
- Feature and pricing comparisons
- Market share and competitive dynamics
- Competitive intelligence gathering

### Customer Research (`customer/`)
- Customer interviews and surveys
- User behavior analysis and insights
- Customer persona development
- Journey mapping and experience analysis
- Feedback analysis and synthesis

### AI Research (`ai/`)
- AI-generated market analysis
- Multi-model research synthesis
- Automated competitive intelligence
- Trend analysis and predictions
- Strategic insights from AI models

### Trend Analysis (`trends/`)
- Industry trend identification
- Technology trend analysis
- Future scenario planning
- Disruption analysis and implications
- Innovation and opportunity spotting

## Document Types
- Market research: `market-[topic]-[date].md`
- Competitive analysis: `competitive-[focus]-[date].md`
- Customer research: `customer-[method]-[date].md`
- AI research: `[date]-[ai-model]-[topic].md`
- Trend analysis: `trends-[industry]-[timeframe].md`

## Research Integration
- Link research to strategic decisions
- Reference in business model updates
- Support product development decisions
- Inform marketing and sales strategies
- Guide investment and funding decisions
